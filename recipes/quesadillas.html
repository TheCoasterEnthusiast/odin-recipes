Quesadillas

Cheese in a tortilla. There's really not much to it.

Ingredients

  • Quesillo
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas on a comal or in a pan, face down at first. Once starting to firm up a little, flip tortilla onto its back.
  2. Fill tortilla with shredded quesillo.
  3. Cook quesadilla until desired crispiness. Serve with salsa.
