Tacos Dorados

Tacos dorados are a classic "antojito". Also known as flautas, they can contain many different fillings and can be topped with many different salsas. This recipe will use potato and chorizo as a filling. A recommended salsa for this dish is salsa verde. Serves two.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of tortilla
  • 1/4 kg of chorizo (Mexican)
  • 3 small potatoes
  • A small chunk of lettuce
  • 1/4 kg of queso panela or queso de aro
  • 1 lime
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise (preferably with lime)

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes and boil them.
  2. Chop lettuce finely and desinfect.
  3. In a small frying pan, cook chorizo.
  4. Once completely cooked, mash potatoes together with chorizo.
  5. Heat a good amount of oil in a large frying pan. Roll tortillas with the potato/chorizo filling and fry, rotating the taco every couple minutes. Remove once crispy.
  6. Use spatula to cover all sides of the taco with mayonnaise. Top with rinsed lettuce, cheese, and salsa verde.
